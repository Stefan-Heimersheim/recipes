# Quark with Mandarins

## Ingredients

* 500g mandarins (canned)
* 6 tbsp mandarin juice
* 750g quark (low-fat)
* 200g sugar
* 1 cup whipping cream
* 1 pkg vanilla sugar

## Instructions

* Drain the mandarins, reserve the juice
* Whip the cream with vanilla sugar
* Mix quark and sugar, fold in the cream and mandarins
