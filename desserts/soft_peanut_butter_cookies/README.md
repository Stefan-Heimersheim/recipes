# Soft Peanut Butter Cookies

*Thanks to Sarah for finding & demonstrating the recipe in the Clare Hall virtual baking-together! Original source: https://cafedelites.com/soft-peanut-butter-cookies/*

## Ingredients

Makes ca. 16 big cookies.

* 65g butter (unsalted)
* 65g peanut butter (creamy, and not pure)
* 85g brown sugar
* 65g white sugar
* 2 tea sp. vanilla essence
* 1 egg
* 192g flour
* 1/2 tea sp. baking powder
* 70g chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. Pre-heat the oven to 175Â°C.
2. Mix butter and peanut butter until creamy.
3. Add sugars and vanilla essence, mix well.
4. Add egg and mix.
5. Add flour and baking powder, fold in (mix but slower, "unterheben").
6. Leave 30% of the chocolate chips for decoration, fold in the rest.
7. Form the dough into 16 balls, put on a tray, press down a bit and add the remaining chocolate chips.
8. Put in the oven for 11 minutes (will be quite soft when coming out).
9. Cover with a cloth for 5 minutes and best eat warm.
