# Herrencreme (Gentleman's Cream)

## Ingredients

* 1.25L milk
* 3 packets pudding powder
* 80g sugar
* 1 cup cream
* 150g chocolate bar
* 5-6cl rum

## Instructions

* Cook the pudding powder with the milk and sugar
* Let cool (best overnight)
* Whip 1 cup of cream
* Beat the pudding with a mixer
* Stir in the rum
* Grate the chocolate and fold it together with the cream into the pudding
