# Paprika Garlic Butter

## Ingredients

* Good margarine (Rama), 250g
* Garlic, 2 cloves
* Tomato paste, to color (ca. 30g)
* Chives (preferably fresh)
* Paprika (ground)
* Maggi seasoning No. 4
* Salt, pepper

## Instructions

* Mix everything with a fork
* Season well with salt
* Add the chives last
