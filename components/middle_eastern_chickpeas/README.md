# Middle Eastern Chickpeas (Balila)

*Source: Recommended recipe from GPT 5.2 Thinking*

Warm lemon-garlic chickpeas. Serves 2 as a side or 1 as a light main.

## Ingredients

* 1 can chickpeas (200g), drained and rinsed
* 1-2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
* 1 clove garlic, finely grated/minced
* 1/2 tsp ground cumin (optional)
* 1/4 tsp paprika (or chili flakes)
* 2 tbsp lemon juice
* salt, black pepper

## Instructions

* Warm chickpeas in a small pot with a splash of water for 3-5 minutes
* Add olive oil, garlic, cumin, paprika, salt and pepper, stir for 30-60 seconds (don't scorch the garlic)
* Turn off heat, add lemon juice, adjust seasoning to taste
