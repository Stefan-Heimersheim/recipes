# Refried Beans

*Source: [BBC Good Food, Amanda Nicolas](https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/recipes/refried-beans)*


## Ingredients

### Refried Beans

* 200g can black, pinto or kidney beans
* 0.5 - 1 onion, finely chopped
* 1 green chilli, chopped (optional)
* 1 garlic clove, finely chopped
* 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
* 1/4 tsp dried oregano
* 1/4 tsp smoked paprika
* salt

### Pico de Gallo

* 1 tomato, diced
* 0.25 white onion, finely diced
* 1 tbsp fresh coriander, chopped
* lime juice (or 0.5 lime)
* salt

## Instructions

### Refried Beans

* Fry the onion in oil until soft and golden (~8 minutes), add garlic, chilli and cumin seeds
* Lightly drain the beans (no rinse), reserving around 75ml juice (a typical jar has around 150ml of juice)
* Mash the beans roughly (in a bowl or in the pan)
* Add beans, oregano, paprika and salt, cook until heated through

### Pico de Gallo

* Mix everything together and season to taste
