# German Pancakes (Pfannkuchen)

## Ingredients

* 250g flour
* 4 eggs
* 4 tbsp sugar
* 1 pinch of salt
* 375 ml milk
* 125 ml sparkling water

## Instructions

* Mix everything together
* Cook in a frying pan with sufficient margarine or butter
* Flip after the bottom side has a nice colour
* Sugar or chocolate cream can be added for serving

### Advanced Version

Drop morello cherries or apples on the dough as soon as it is in the pan, be careful when flipping.
