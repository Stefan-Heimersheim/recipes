# Pasta Caprese

*Mozzarella-tomato pasta from Campania, Italy*

## Ingredients (for 4 people)

* 300g very aromatic tomatoes
* 2 cloves of garlic
* 1-2 dried pepperoncini
* 250g mozzarella (preferably buffalo mozzarella)
* 4 tbsp olive oil
* 400g penne or macaroni
* Salt
* 1 bunch basil
* 1 small can tuna in olive oil (drained weight ca. 50g)
* 1 tbsp small capers

## Instructions (ca. 25 min)

* Wash the tomatoes and cut into cubes, removing the stem ends. Peel the garlic and chop finely. Crush the pepperoncini finely. Dice the mozzarella into about 1cm cubes.
* Mix tomatoes, garlic, pepperoncini and mozzarella with the olive oil and let marinate for about 1 hour.
* Bring plenty of water to a boil for the pasta and add salt. Cook the pasta al dente according to package instructions.
* Meanwhile, remove the basil leaves from the stems and tear into pieces. Drain and flake the tuna.
* Drain the pasta and return to the pot. Mix in the mozzarella mixture with basil, tuna and capers, season with salt and let stand covered for 1-2 min in the hot pot on the turned-off burner. Serve on pre-warmed plates.
