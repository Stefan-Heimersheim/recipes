# Chicken Fricassee with Rice [WIP]

## Ingredients

* Chicken meat, bone-in is better for the broth (you can buy half-and-half)
* Soup vegetables
* Onions
* Salt, pepper, Maggi, Worcestershire sauce
* Chicken broth
* Cream

## Instructions

* Cook the chicken (wash beforehand if needed) in a large pot together with soup vegetables and onions, also add salt, pepper and some chicken broth
* After 1-2 hours you can take out the meat, it should come off the bones easily
* Make a roux with 2-3 tbsp margarine and 2-3 tbsp flour (rather a bit more)
* Add about half of the chicken broth (save the other half for next time)
* Add cream
* Add the meat
* Season to taste
