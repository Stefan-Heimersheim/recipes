# Amazing Soy Bolognese

Hearty vegan bolognese. Serves 3-4.

## Ingredients

* 500g soy-based mince (e.g. Tesco Plant Chef)
* 1 jar bolognese sauce
* 1 can chopped tomatoes
* 1 can lentils, drained
* 1.5 onions, finely chopped
* Black pepper, lots
* 1/2-1 tsp smoked paprika (or sweet)
* Pinch of chilli flakes
* 1/2-1 tsp dried oregano
* 1 bay leaf
* 1/2 tsp dried thyme (or a few fresh sprigs)
* Small pinch of rosemary

## Instructions (ca. 30 min)

* Fry onions in oil until soft, ca. 5 min
* Add mince, brown for ca. 10 min
* Add bolognese sauce, chopped tomatoes and lentils
* Season with pepper, paprika, chilli flakes, oregano, thyme, rosemary
* Drop in the bay leaf
* Simmer for ca. 15 min, stirring occasionally
* Remove bay leaf before serving
