# Flammkuchen (Tarte Flambée)

## Ingredients (for 4 trays)

### Dough
* 4 tbsp oil
* 250 mL water
* some salt (2 pinches)
* 500g flour

### Topping
* 2 cups crème fraîche
* 2 cups sour cream (Schmand)
* Pepper
* Salt

### Alsatian Style (2 trays)
* 2 onions (thinly sliced into rings)
* 200g bacon (diced)
* Chive rolls

### Salmon (1 tray)
* 150g smoked salmon
* 1 onion
* Chive rolls
* Dill

### Fig & Feta (1 tray)
* ca. 10 dried figs
* 150g feta
* 1 onion

## Instructions

* Preheat oven to 250°C
* Knead the dough and roll out very thin
* Mix sour cream and crème fraîche, season and spread on the dough
* Distribute the remaining topping on the flammkuchen
* Bake for ca. 10 min (until the edges are lightly browned) on the lower rack
