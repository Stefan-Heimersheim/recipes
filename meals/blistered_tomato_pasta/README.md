# Blistered Tomato + Chickpea Pasta

*Source: Recommended by GPT 5.2*

Quick jammy tomato pasta with chickpeas. Serves 2-3.

## Ingredients

* Pasta for 2-3
* 300-500g tomatoes, chopped/halved
* 1 tin chickpeas, drained (rinse if you want)
* 3-4 tbsp olive oil
* 3 cloves garlic, sliced
* Salt, black pepper
* Chilli flakes, paprika/cumin (optional)
* Basil/parsley, parmesan/mozarella/feta, lemon (optional)

## Instructions (ca. 15-20 min)

* Boil pasta in salted water, reserve 1/2-1 cup pasta water
* Heat oil in a big pan on medium-high, add (chopped) tomatoes + a good pinch of salt
* Cook 6-8 min until tomatoes burst and turn jammy
* Add chickpeas + a pinch of paprika (and/or cumin), cook 3-4 min
* Add garlic + chilli flakes, cook 30-60 sec
* Add pasta + a splash of pasta water, toss 1-2 min until glossy (add more water as needed)
* Finish with pepper, lemon zest/juice and herbs, cheese if you like
