# Rice Curry Pan

## Ingredients

* 400g rice
* 400g chicken
* 1 can peaches
* Some (frozen) peas
* 2-3 eggs
* Milk
* Onions, garlic, ginger
* Curry, salt, pepper

## Instructions

* Cook 600g rice with 1.3l water and 30g salt
* Cut peaches, reserve the juice
* Fry meat (chicken) and onions (and optionally garlic) in oil, season with salt and pepper
* Add rice, continue frying
* Add 2-3 eggs (not as fried eggs but also not completely mixed in)
* Add milk and peach juice
* Season with curry, chilli, salt, pepper, ginger, ...
* Add (frozen) peas
* Add peaches and let them warm up
